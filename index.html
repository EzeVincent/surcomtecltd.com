Welcome to Surcomtec Ltd...

Surcomtec Ltd - Top security Equipement service company in Nigeria

Surcomtec Limited, a subsidiary of SURCOMTEC GROUP was established and fully incorporated in 1993 as a professional total electronic security solution services company.

Surcomtec limited focuses on delivering superb security solutions and services and also engages on corporate community services through supporting of local Police, particularly in the communities from which it draws personnel.

Surcomtec limited core business is the provision of efficient and cost-effective total security solutions supported by state-of-the-art Electronic Security Systems through professionals and highly trained personnel, utilizing international standards and local knowledge. The company has expertise in the areas of all electronic security system, 24hrs remote electronic alarm monitoring (for robbery, burglary and fire alarm with ARMED POLICE PATROL AND RESPONSE), risk management and security surveys and consultancy.

Surcomtec limited follows a business approach which ensures customers’ needs are understood, standards are set to measure achievement of objectives and focus is directed towards critical success factors and innovation.
